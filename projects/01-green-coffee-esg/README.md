# Project 01 – Green Coffee ESG Intelligence

## 1. Context
European green coffee importers face increasing ESG pressure related to
traceability, climate risk and supplier performance.

## 2. Objective
Design a data-driven ESG intelligence framework for green coffee sourcing
that supports strategic and regulatory decision-making.

## 3. Scope
- Sector: Green coffee
- Geography: Latin America → Spain (EU)
- Stakeholders: Importers, roasters, ESG teams

## 4. Data Sources
- FAOSTAT
- Global Coffee Platform benchmarks
- World Bank indicators

## 5. Analytical Approach
Exploratory data analysis, ESG indicators construction and comparative benchmarking.

## 6. Outputs
- ESG scoring framework
- Visual dashboards
- Strategic insights

## 7. Value Generated
Provides a structured way to assess sustainability risks and opportunities
across coffee supply chains.
